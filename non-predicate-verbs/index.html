非谓语动词

发表于 2022-04-04 更新于 2022-04-26 分类于 非谓语动词

非谓语动词。

动名词

动名词做非谓语动词时可以在句子中做除了谓语和系动词外的所有六种成分。

主语 S

以动词短语形式出现

例： Eating herbs is good for your health.

前接形物代或所有格

例： Tom’s（形物代）/His（所有格） eating too much made him sick.

It is no …

It is no + 名词（通常为good或use）+ doing sth.

例： It’s no use saying that much.

宾语 O

做及物动词的直宾

例： I like playing football.

it做形宾，动名词做实宾

例： I found it no use doing that much.

介宾结构

例： We learned the key to living together.

前接名词、所有格、人称代词宾格或形物代

总结为 me my Tom Tom’s，分别对应形物代、代词宾格、名词和名词所有格。

例： Would you mind me/my/Tom/Tom’s smoking here?

表语 P

例： Seeing is believing.

定语 At

单独出现

单独出现时位于所修饰词之前。

例： There’s a living fish.

作为短语出现

作为短语出现时位于所修饰词之后。

例： There’s a boy (who is) standing under the tree.

状语 Ad

同时发生的动作

例： Holding a pen, the teacher came into the class.

连贯发生的动作

例： Opening the door, the teacher came into the class.

动名词短语表结果

例：

  • The flood went away, causing a lot of damage.
  • The sun rose up, bathing the whole mountain in golden rays.
  • The glass door took the place of the wooden one, letting the sunlight in.

较长一段时间后发生的事

Having done 位于句首。

例： Having shown them around the campus, the headmaster head them to the canteen.

补语 C

看见某人做某事

结构为 see sb. doing sth.

例： Standing by the window, I found him stealing flowers in the garden.

​ = I stood by the window, finding him stealing flowers in the garden.

动词不定式

与动名词一样，动词不定式做非谓语动词时可以在句子中做除了谓语和系动词外的所有六种成分。

主语 S

直接做主语

例： To go to THU is my dream.

it做形式主语，动名词做实际主语

结构为 It is + adj. + for/of sb. + to do sth.
其中for指外在，of指内在。

例：

  • It‘s wise of you to say that.
  • It‘s good for you to eat this.

宾语 O

做及物动词的直宾

例： （暂无例句）

it做形宾，动名词做实宾

通常结构为 find/think/believe + it + adj. + to do sth.

例： I find it difficult for me to finish it on time.

表语 P

例： My dream is to go to THU.

定语 At

不定式做定语时一般后置。

例：

  • I have a lot of homework to do.
  • I have a good way to solve the problem.

状语 Ad

目的

to do 表目的棵译作“为了”。

太简单了不给例句。

原因

例：

  • (I’m) nice to meet you.
  • I’m sorry to hear that.
  • I’m sorry to inform you that …

结果

never

例： …, never to be found.

only

only 表示与主观意愿相反的结果。

例： The reporter hurried to the station, only to find that the stars had gone.

too… to…

太简单了不给例句。

be adj. enough to do sth.

太简单了不给例句。

条件

结构为 主语 + be + adj. + to do sth.

其中主语一般为名词，而形容词一般描述主语的某种性质或功能。

例： He is easy to get along with.

动词过去分词

动词过去分词做非谓语动词时，在句子中除了不能做谓语和系动词外，还不能做主语和宾语。

下面就只有例句了。

表语 P

1

例： He is excited/interested/bored.

2.

例： The cup is broken.

定语 At

前置

例： Look at his excited face.

后置

例： I like the cup bought by my friend.

状语 Ad

例： Scolded by his teacher, he …

补语 C

例： Please speak louder to make yourself heard.
